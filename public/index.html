About

Stars and the Underground is the musical project of Auckland, New Zealand based singer songwriter John Mason. (ex Winterland circa 1999)

Formed in 2019, the band has released an EP "Evolution Circle" and two singles to date, "Nosferatu Upon Tyne" (2020) and "Lonely Black Hearts" (2025) both to critical acclaim. The band is currently working on their debut album "Guardians of the Night".

The band has performed live in New Zealand and Australia with a variety of line ups and guest musicians. The current line up is John Mason (Vocals/Guitar) and John McDermott (Drums).

Stars and the Underground's music is a blend of Post-Punk, Shoegaze and Dark Synthwave with influences from bands such as The Cure, The Church, The Mission, Joy Division, Depeche Mode, The Chameleons, Flying Colors, My Bloody Valentine, Drab Majesty, Twin Tribes and Kælan Mikla.
